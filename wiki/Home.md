## About

This plugin allows OBS Studio users to display real-time Xbox Live data in their streams:
- **Xbox Account** - Authenticate with your Microsoft account
- **Xbox Game Cover** - Show the cover art of your currently playing game
- **Xbox Gamerscore** - Display your total gamerscore
- **Achievement Tracking** - Monitor achievement unlocks (coming soon)

## Platform Support

The plugin supports:
- **Windows** - Full support
- **macOS** - Full support (both Intel and Apple Silicon)
- **Linux** - Full support

## Quick Installation

1. Download the latest release for your platform
2. Extract to your OBS plugins directory
3. Restart OBS Studio
4. Add an Xbox source and sign in with your Microsoft account

For detailed instructions, see the [User Guide](User-Guide.md).
